# Inspiration — Full Page References

Full-page screenshots for overall vibe, aesthetic, and feel. These are not component references — Claude uses these for overall tone and style direction, not for specific pattern lookup.

## What to put here
- Full-page screenshots of products/sites with an aesthetic you want to emulate
- Brand references with strong visual identity
- "This is the feel I'm going for" references

## What NOT to put here
- Component-specific screenshots → those go in /patterns
- Company screenshots organized by company → organize by pattern instead

## How Claude uses this folder
When starting a new project or establishing aesthetic direction, Claude may review this folder to understand overall style intent. This is context, not prescription.
